A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favourite websites. Open a new browser tan and see your sites load instantly. Try it for free

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time t all. No need to trawl through all of your bookmarks

Share your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with other. Create a shareble link that you can send at the click of a button
